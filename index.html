About Squidward

Squidward Quincy tentacles ,born on october 9th Squidward is a talented artist and has a degree in art from Bikini Bottom Art college

Squidward is known for his grumpy and sarcastic personality , as well as his love of arts , particularly playing the clarinet

despite his grumpy demeanor , squidward cares deeply for his friends and has been known to go out of his way to help them when they need it

His negative attitude and social isolation suggest he may be struggling with his mental health
